## Research finding

Mistral OCR 4 was released June 23, 2026 and includes bounding boxes, block classification, inline confidence scores, 170-language support, and self-hosted deployment in a single container. Mistral prices the API at $4/1,000 pages, with batch discount to $2/1,000 pages. ([Mistral AI][20])
